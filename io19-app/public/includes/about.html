About MAT I/O

A Great Welcoming Party for MAT Students.
MAT I/O is an annual welcoming party event for all students of Mobile Application and Technology. We believe that every one of us should excel through innovation and togetherness. This event is open for active MAT students of all classes and semesters. Even some of your favourite lecturers may come to I/O as well!

Connecting the Dots
This year, our theme is, as you have guessed it, "Connecting the Dots". Just like the theme, we hope that this event can help you to build networks and connections to more people, including MAT students and professionals.

Innovation Included
Of course, this event would not be called "I/O" without showcasing innovation. Beware of our new students who will present their great ideas as part of MAT Ideathon, their first contest of mobile app concept organized by us. They could present crazy, wild ideas that can change Indonesia and the world. Furthermore, they will be finally judged to determine the true winners of the contest.

Finally, Performances!
This event would not be more exciting without performances. Stay tuned for more information of our performers lists!
