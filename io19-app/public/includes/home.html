MAT I/O is an annual welcoming party event for Mobile Application & Technology students. MAT Ideathon

A competition for new MAT intakes to show their ideas.

MAT Ideathon is a place where new intakes can present their innovative ideas to make an impact for the world, especially for Indonesia. MAT Ideathon challenges all contestants to be competitive, innovative and can give an impact for the world. Our focus in this competition is education, health and social.
